Telangana ECET Exam Overview

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must be Indian nationals and residents of Telangana. They should have passed the Diploma in Engineering/Technology or B.Sc. (Mathematics) degree examination with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).

Exam Pattern

The exam is conducted online and consists of multiple-choice questions. It covers subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Engineering subjects specific to the chosen branch. The total marks and duration vary depending on the branch.

Syllabus

The syllabus includes topics from Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and core engineering subjects relevant to the chosen branch. Detailed syllabus can be found in the official notification.
